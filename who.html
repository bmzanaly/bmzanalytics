Brandon M. Zagorski, MS, P.Stat. is the Principal and Managing Director of BMZ Analytics and is Adjunct Professor at the University of Toronto, Faculty of Medicine, Institute of Health Policy, Management and Evaluation. Brandon’s professional experience, spanning almost 20 years across multiple research disciplines and industry sectors, provides the unique opportunity to quickly understand most analytical needs of directors and managers and to identify realistic data science solutions. Brandon further distinguishes himself by his clear and effective communication with investigators, efficient problem solving, and delivering quality services.

Brandon is also actively engaged in teaching applied statistical principles to burgeoning scientists in the Institute of Health Policy, Management and Evaluation at the University of Toronto.
